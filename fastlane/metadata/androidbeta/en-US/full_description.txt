<p><i>Ownkey Keyboard Beta</i> is the preview channel for Ownkey Keyboard.</p>

<p>You get early access to new improvements around:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Mistral Voxtral ASR voice input</li>
  <li>BYOK configuration and endpoint flexibility</li>
  <li>Autocorrect quality and typing suggestions</li>
  <li>UI and design refinements</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Current direction:</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Open-source Android keyboard</li>
  <li>Privacy-first behavior</li>
  <li>No in-app monitoring of private user content</li>
</ul>

<p>Beta builds can contain unfinished features. If you want stable behavior, use the stable track once available.</p>

<p>If cloud ASR is enabled, requests are sent to your configured endpoint.</p>
